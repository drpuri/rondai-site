The Problem

Technology should work for clinicians —
not the other way around.

You round at one facility Monday, another Tuesday, a third Wednesday. Between buildings, you're tracking pending orders, prior auths, family callbacks, and follow-ups — pulling information out of the EMR and organizing it your own way.

Everyone does it a little differently. Different information, different structure, different order. You've spent years building a personalized system to make sure nothing slips through the cracks.

That system lives in your head, on paper, in spreadsheets. It's manual, it's personal, and nothing in healthcare is designed to support it. The tools exist — but they're not built around how you actually work, and they don't remember your preferences.

How Rondai Works

You talk to it like a person.
It remembers like one too.

No EHR integration required. Ronda learns from you — and gets smarter the longer you use her.

1

Tell it what's happening

After rounds, tell Rondai what you ordered, what's pending, who needs a callback. Like texting an MA — in your own words, your own format.

2

3

A Day With Rondai

Tuesday morning, before rounds.

Your phone buzzes. Rondai already knows what you need.

6:30 AM

Mrs. Chen, Room 14 — CMP results should be back from Monday's order. Cardiology note still outstanding. Daughter called Monday about the medication change, hasn't heard back.

6:31 AM

Mr. Rodriguez, Room 8 — Aetna prior auth for Eliquis expires Friday. Based on your history, Rondai has drafted the renewal. Approve or review?

6:32 AM

You reply: "Approve Eliquis. Remind me to call Chen's daughter after 4pm." Done. You walk in already knowing everything.

What Makes Rondai Different

Cross-facility continuity

Rondai maintains one picture of what's pending across every building you cover. Monday's order at one facility connects to Wednesday's follow-up at another automatically.

Preference learning

After a few weeks, Rondai knows you want potassium before adjusting Lasix, that you auto-approve certain prior auths, and that you call families after 4pm. It stops asking what it already knows.

Portable
