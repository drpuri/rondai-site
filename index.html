The Problem

Technology should work for clinicians —
not the other way around.

You're at the hospital in the morning, a clinic by lunch, rounding at a facility in the afternoon. Between locations, you're tracking pending orders, prior auths, family callbacks, and follow-ups — pulling from different EMRs and organizing it your own way.

That system lives in your head, on paper, in spreadsheets. It's manual, it's personal, and nothing in healthcare is designed to support it. You already have a team — but no one person sees your whole day across every location. Ronda does.

How Ronda Works

You teach her your workflows.
She runs them.

Ronda doesn't come pre-built for one way of working. You shape her by talking to her — and she turns what you say into skills she runs automatically.

1

Tell her how you work

"Every Monday before I round, check which patients have new lab results and list them by location." Ronda turns that into a workflow she runs every Monday — no code, no setup.

2

Connect your tools

Link your email, calendar, EMR, and communication platforms to give Ronda more context. The more she sees, the more she can do on her own.

3

She gets smarter over time

Every conversation teaches her something. Your preferences, your shortcuts, your patterns. Over time she stops asking and starts doing.
